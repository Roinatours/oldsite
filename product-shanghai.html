Seven-Day Cultural Tour

A Journey into Jiangnan Culture — Clothing, Food, Housing, and Transportation

Download for Details

Join our seven-day cultural tour through the Jiangnan region (Shanghai, Zhejiang, and Jiangsu), where you'll dive into an immersive exploration of this iconic water town's lifestyle! With the theme of "Ancient to Modern, Day to Night," you will explore the unique charm of Jiangnan, known for its scenic beauty, traditional customs, and modern advancements.

During the opening ceremony, you and your fellow travelers will embark on a week-long adventure themed around a "Who's the Spy?" game and "Jiangnan Cultural Encounters." Each day, we will visit different landmarks and historical sites, diving into the essence of Jiangnan's cultural and natural beauty—from the delicate design of Suzhou's gardens to the serene landscapes of Hangzhou's West Lake, from the bustling night view of Shanghai's Bund to the rustic charm of Nanxiang Ancient Town.

Through hands-on experiences such as "Rice Cake Making," "Suzhou Embroidery," "Indigo Dyeing," and "Yue Opera Classes," participants will not only acquire knowledge of traditional Chinese culture but also develop a deeper appreciation for the unique allure of the Jiangnan region through practice and engagement.

Clothing, Food, Housing, Transportation

Program Breakdown

Clothing

Fields of mulberry and hemp — traditional clothing culture across the region.

  • Suzhou: Embroidery Workshop, Garden Hanfu Experience
  • Hangzhou: Indigo Dyeing, Yue Opera Lessons

Food

Rice paddies and fish ponds — Jiangnan's famous local delicacies.

  • Shanghai: Nanxiang dumplings, rice cakes, and more
  • Suzhou: Aozao noodles, osmanthus lotus seeds
  • Hangzhou: Longjing tea, West Lake vinegar fish

Housing

White walls and black tiles — the region's traditional living environments.

  • Shanghai: International architecture, Nanxiang / Zhujiajiao Ancient Town
  • Suzhou: Classical gardens
  • Hangzhou: Liangzhu culture

Transportation

Boats as transport — from ancient waterways to modern innovation.

  • Shanghai: The Bund Ferry, Baidu Autonomous Vehicles, Tesla / Maglev Experience
  • Suzhou: Qilishantang boat rides
