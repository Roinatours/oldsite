西安 · Xi'an

A Journey Through Time

An ancient capital, embodies the brilliance of Chinese civilization with its rich history and profound cultural heritage.

Seven-Day Study Tour

Theme: The Evolution of Historical Dynasties  ·  Sub-theme: Ancient Traditional Timekeeping Methods

Download for Details

Join us for a seven-day study tour in Xi'an, where you'll experience a cultural feast that brings you face to face with history! The main theme revolves around the evolution of Chinese dynasties, with the sub-theme focusing on ancient traditional timekeeping methods—specifically, the Twelve Earthly Branches. We will guide you through 5,000 years of Chinese civilization, exploring the brilliance and uniqueness of each era.

At the opening ceremony, you will receive a seven-day challenge card to embark on this journey filled with exploration and discovery. Each day, we will visit different historical sites to gain insights into the Neolithic civilization and the spark of fire; the remarkable achievements of the Qin Dynasty and the mysteries of the Terracotta Army; the prosperity and cultural fusion of the Tang Dynasty; the brilliance and charm of shadow puppetry in the Ming Dynasty; as well as modern cave dwellings and the inheritance of folk arts.

Through hands-on experiences such as "Fire by Friction," "DIY Terracotta Warriors," "Hanfu Styling," and "Shadow Puppet Making," you will not only acquire knowledge but also feel the pulse of history in practice. Finally, you will showcase your talents on the Xi'an City Wall, sharing unforgettable memories and insights from this journey.
