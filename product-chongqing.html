重庆 · Chongqing

Five Senses Exploration

A city of mountains and rivers, is famous for its unique hot pot culture and stunning night views.

Seven-Day Study Tour

Journey through the Mountain City

Download for Details

Join our seven-day study tour in Chongqing, where you will embark on an immersive sensory experience with nature and culture! Centered around the five senses and branching out to Chongqing's rich humanistic and natural landscapes, we will take you deep into the unique charm of this mountain city.

At the opening ceremony, you will receive a seven-day challenge card with your fellow travelers, kicking off a journey full of exploration and discovery. Each day, we will visit different scenic spots and historical sites, using your five senses to gain a deeper understanding of Chongqing's natural wonders and cultural essence. From the intricate carvings of the Dazu Rock Carvings to the brilliant night scenes of Hongya Cave; from the ancient atmosphere of Ciqikou Old Town to the dramatic art of Sichuan Opera's face-changing performance.

Through hands-on experiences like "Pattern Rubbing," "Bridge Design," "Hotpot Tasting," and "Sichuan Opera Makeup," you will not only gain knowledge but also feel the unique charm of Chongqing in practice. At the end, you will present your poster at Jiefangbei Pedestrian Street, sharing your unforgettable journey and insights.

Day by Day

Activity Content

Day 1 — Jiefangbei

Visit Jiefangbei Pedestrian Street to explore its origins, finding a historical plaque that explains its significance. Builds historical knowledge, observation skills, and recording & expression.

Day 2 — Dazu Rock Carvings

Appreciate the stone carving art at the Dazu Rock Carvings, finding a specific Buddha statue and reflecting on the carving. Builds art appreciation, cultural understanding, and critical thinking.

Day 3 — Chongqing Hotpot

Taste Chongqing hotpot and find a menu describing its history and unique ingredients. Builds culinary culture knowledge, sensory experience, and recording & analysis skills.

Day 4 — Hongya Cave

Enjoy Chongqing's night view at Hongya Cave, photographing the scenery and describing a favorite building. Builds aesthetic appreciation, photography, and observation & description skills.

Day 5 — Sichuan Opera

Attend a Sichuan Opera face-changing performance, finding the signature of a face-changing master. Builds knowledge of theatrical art, interactive experience, and expression skills.

Day 6 — Ciqikou Old Town

Experience local customs at Ciqikou Old Town, finding a traditional handicraft and learning its production process. Builds folk culture knowledge, craftsmanship, and cultural heritage appreciation.

Day 7 — Final Sharing

Organize clues from the previous six days and return to Jiefangbei for a final sharing, creating a collage and presenting discoveries. Builds summarization skills, teamwork, and confidence.
